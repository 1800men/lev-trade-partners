Who we are

An independent partner in global energy.

Lev Trade Partners is an independent physical trading and distribution house specializing in base oils, petrochemicals, and refined products. We connect dependable supply with growing demand, and we arrange the financing, logistics, and storage so our partners can focus on their business.

"Securing the right product, in the right place, at the right time. That is the whole of our craft."

Our work rests on three commitments at the core of how we operate: long-term relationships, dependable supply, and a team of experienced professionals.

Long-term relationships

Partnerships built on trust, performance, and presence, not single trades.

Absolute supply security

Diversified sourcing and trusted terminal partners so cargoes arrive in full and on schedule.

Experienced team

An international team with strong market knowledge and a growing partner network.

How we work

From first enquiry to delivered cargo.

A clear, transparent procedure. You always know the next step and who is responsible for it.

01

Enquiry

You send product, grade, volume, destination port, and terms through our enquiry form.

02

Soft offer

We issue a soft corporate offer with price, specification, and Incoterms, typically within 48 hours.

03

Contract

We agree terms and sign a sales and purchase agreement, with clear obligations on both sides.

04

Inspection

Independent inspection at load and discharge, with full bankable documentation.

05

Delivery

Cargo moves under the agreed Incoterms and settles by the agreed payment instrument.

Standards & assurance

Built to pass your compliance check.

We run every transaction the way a serious counterparty expects, so your bank and your auditors are comfortable from day one.

Independent inspection

SGS or Intertek verify quality and quantity at load and discharge, before payment is released.

Full documentation

COA, SGS report, Bill of Lading, certificate of origin, packing list. Bankable documents for your LC.

Secure settlement

LC, SBLC, DLC, or TT, structured to protect both parties under Incoterms 2020.

Compliance & KYC

Full due diligence both ways. We share proof of product, and we ask for proof of funds. No exceptions.
